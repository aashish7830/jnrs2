JNRS HOSPITALITY GROUP

JNRS Hospitality Group, through its group companies Dazzling Hospitality Management Private Limited and JNRS Infrastructure Private Limited, based in Mumbai, is moving forward towards a significant new milestone in the hospitality industry.

Under the JNRS banner, the group is introducing a curated chain of 3-star and 4-star hotels, resorts, cottages, and boutique villas across India. As part of this expansion, the group is proudly launching premium properties such as JNRS Royal Park Resort Srinagar, JNRS Wooden Cottage Pahalgam, and JNRS Wooden Villa Shimla.

A Vision for 2026–2027

In line with its strategic vision for 2026–2027, JNRS aims to launch hotels and resorts in 10 of India’s most popular vacation destinations. Through this expansion, the group plans to build an inventory of more than 1,000 rooms, catering to its valued B2B travel partners, travel companies, corporate clients, and guests across the country.
